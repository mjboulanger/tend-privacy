Privacy Policy for Tend

Last updated: May 22, 2026


Overview
Tend ("the app") is a household management app. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Data We Collect

 - Account information: Your name and email address, collected via Sign in with Apple
 - Household data: Tasks, recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists you create within the app
 - Device information: Basic device data collected by Apple as part of standard app operation

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality — syncing your household's tasks, recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists across devices. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Analytics

The app does not currently use any analytics or tracking tools. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing martinjboulanger@gmail.com. We will action all requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Tend is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at martinjboulanger@gmail.com.
